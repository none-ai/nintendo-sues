Nintendo Sues the US Government

TechCrunch | Latest News | March 2026

Welcome to the Nintendo Tariff Lawsuit Coverage

Nintendo has filed a lawsuit against the US government seeking a refund on tariffs imposed on imported video game consoles and accessories. This landmark case could have significant implications for the video game industry and international trade relations.

What This Means

The lawsuit challenges tariffs that Nintendo claims were incorrectly applied to its products. The company seeks a refund of millions of dollars in tariffs paid over the past several years.
